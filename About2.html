Community Covenant

This document serves as a summary of the shared responsibilities and professional ethics involved in being a member of our community.

As an expression of our sincere desire to collaborate effectively, we pledge ourselves to maintain high standards of professional conduct and mutual respect within this organization.

We believe that integrity is vital. We promise to watch over our collective reputation and discourage any conduct that does not align with our core mission and values.

We acknowledge the importance of transparent communication and regular feedback. Our concern is to support the growth of every individual within the collective framework.

We resolve to maintain unity by following internal mediation protocols to address grievances. We aim to solve all internal disputes with gentleness, patience, and professional courtesy.

Members recognize the responsibility of supporting the organization through participation, stewardship of shared tools, and adhering to financial transparency policies.
